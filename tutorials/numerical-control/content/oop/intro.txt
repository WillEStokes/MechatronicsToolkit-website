<p>Welcome to the world of object-oriented programming (OOP) in C++! Object-oriented programming is a programming paradigm that allows developers to model real-world objects and their interactions. It is a way of organizing and structuring code, making it easier to maintain, understand, and extend. In this tutorial, we will delve into the fundamental concepts of OOP in C++, including classes, objects, inheritance, polymorphism, and access modifiers. By the end of this tutorial, you should have a solid understanding of OOP principles and be able to write your own object-oriented programs in C++. So, let's get started!</p>
